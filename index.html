Container Ships

Container ships are used for transporting standardized containers across the seas.

Oil Tankers

Oil tankers transport large quantities of crude oil or refined products.

RORO Ships

RORO (Roll-On/Roll-Off) ships are designed to carry vehicles and cargo that can be driven on and off.

Heavy Lift Ships

Heavy lift ships are used to transport oversized cargo that cannot be carried by other vessels.

Bulk Carriers

Bulk carriers transport bulk goods like coal, grain, and ore.

Cruise Vessels

Cruise vessels are passenger ships used for pleasure voyages, where the voyage itself and the ship's amenities are a part of the experience.

Expedition Cruise Vessels

Expedition cruise vessels are designed for adventure cruises, often in remote locations like the Arctic or Antarctic.

Expedition Vessels

Expedition vessels are specially equipped for long journeys and exploration missions.

Other Vessels

This category includes various specialized ships like research vessels, icebreakers, and more.
